Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I list my business on YusKar Hub?

Simply click on "Sign Up" and create an account. Once registered, you can add your business listing through your dashboard.

What are the pricing plans?

We offer Free, Professional ($29/month), and Enterprise ($99/month) plans. Each plan includes different features and visibility levels.

How long does it take for my business to be approved?

Business listings are typically reviewed and approved within 24-48 hours during business days.

Can I edit my business listing after submission?

Yes, you can edit your business information anytime through your dashboard. Changes are updated immediately.
