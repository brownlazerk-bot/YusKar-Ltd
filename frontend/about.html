About YusKar Business Hub

Empowering Rwandan Businesses Since 2020

Our Story

YusKar Business Hub was founded with a simple mission: to connect Rwandan businesses with their customers and help them thrive in the digital age. We recognized the need for a centralized platform where local businesses could showcase their products and services to a wider audience.

Since our inception in 2020, we've grown to become Rwanda's most trusted business marketplace, serving over 1,200 businesses and connecting them with thousands of customers across the country.

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, accessible, and effective in helping businesses of all sizes - from small local shops to large enterprises - reach their full potential.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower Rwandan businesses by providing a comprehensive digital platform that enhances visibility, fosters connections, and drives sustainable growth in the local economy.

🔮 Our Vision

To become the leading business marketplace in East Africa, setting the standard for digital business directories and contributing to Rwanda's digital transformation journey.
